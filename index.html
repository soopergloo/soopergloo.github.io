DSGN 1020 Portfolio
Works and Reading Reponses in Art of the Web

Preface

This is a portfolio of my work for DSGN 1020: Art of the Web at the University of Pennsylvania, which I took during the Spring 2024 semester. It comprises various works created in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as responses to readings assigned throughout the semester. This portfolio is meant to imitate the style one might encounter when reading a PDF in Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox (or whatever browser one might use). It is also partly inspired by my interactions in LaTeX, an environment which I have become wholly familiar with in my time at Penn.
